## Slide 7
### Skim Article B
1. Is article B an empirical or conceptual research paper? Explain with reason.
Article B is conceptual research paper. The writing theorizes the potential risks and consequences of using Facial Recognition Technology in schools. The writer did not carry out any real-world research to support his/ her stance.
