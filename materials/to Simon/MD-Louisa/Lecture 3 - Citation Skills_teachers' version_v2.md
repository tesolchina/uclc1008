## Slide 19
### Paraphrasing – Non-author-prominent approach
“Luck can be fascinating if you perceive it as part of risk and chance” (Aitkin, 2011).
### 2. Non-author-prominent (NO signal phrases)
Luck is fascinating when considered part of risk and chance (Aitkin, 2011).
Fullstop at the end of everything!
