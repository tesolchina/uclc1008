### 8. `week4_8_error_analysis.html`
**Learn from Common Errors**

**Activities:**
- **Error Gallery:** Common AWQ errors with examples
- **Error Identification:** Find errors in sample responses
- **Error Correction:** Fix errors in sample responses
- **MC Questions (15):** Identify error types
- **Reflection:** Analyze your own previous errors

**Common Errors to Include:**
- Too close to original (paraphrasing issues)
- Missing citations
- Incorrect citation format
- Including irrelevant details
- Missing key arguments
- Poor structure
- Word count issues

**AI Features:**
- AI error detector: Identify errors in student work
- Feedback: Explanation of errors and how to fix
