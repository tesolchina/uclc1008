# Week 5, Lesson 1: Full Mock AWQ

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
1.  Complete a full **60-minute AWQ simulation**.
2.  Demonstrate mastery of all Week 1-4 skills (Reading, Citation, Paraphrasing, Synthesis).

## Content Breakdown

### 1. The Mock Exam
*   **Prompt:** Summarise, paraphrase and synthesise the main claims from the TWO excerpts regarding "The implementation of FRT in schools". (Using the full Article A and Article B from the Study Guide).
*   **Constraints:**
    *   **Time:** 60 Minutes.
    *   **Length:** Max 300 words.
    *   **Format:** 3 Paragraphs (Intro, Body, Conclusion).

### 2. Instructions
1.  **Read:** Article A (Support) and Article B (Doubt).
2.  **Plan:** Identify common themes (Privacy, Efficiency, Consent).
3.  **Write:** Produce the essay.

## Interactive HTML Elements (Draft Ideas)

### Activity 1: The Full Timer
*   **Type:** 60-Minute Countdown
*   **Display:** Split screen. Texts on the left (scrollable), Writing area on the right.
*   **Features:**
    *   "Time Remaining" bar.
    *   "Word Count" indicator.

### Activity 2: Post-Exam Cool Down
*   **Type:** Checklist
*   **Task:** Immediate post-test check.
    *   Did you finish?
    *   Did you include a Reference List (if required)?
    *   Did you synthesize?

*(Note: Since this is a Mock, the HTML file will primarily be the "Exam Environment".)*

## Key Takeaway
"This is the dress rehearsal. Treat it exactly like the real thing. No phones, no distractions."
