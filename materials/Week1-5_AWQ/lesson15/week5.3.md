# Week 5, Lesson 3: Final Readiness & Checklist

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
1.  Create a personal **Exam Strategy Plan**.
2.  Recall the **"Top 3 Rules"** for the AWQ.
3.  Manage exam stress with prepared routines.

## Content Breakdown

### 1. The Final Checklist (Go/No-Go)
*   [ ] I know how to cite APA 7th (Author & Info prominent).
*   [ ] I know 5 reporting verbs (Argues, Claims, Suggests, Examines, Notes).
*   [ ] I know how to structure the 3 paragraphs.
*   [ ] I know to skip the "Methods" section detailed data.

### 2. Time Management Strategy Recap
*   0-10: Read & Plan.
*   10-50: Write.
*   50-60: Check.

### 3. Final Tips
*   **Don't Panic:** If you don't understand a word, look at the context.
*   **Keep it Simple:** Clear sentences are better than complex, messy ones.
*   **Stick to the Plan:** Don't improvise a new structure on exam day.

## Interactive HTML Elements (Draft Ideas)

### Activity 1: The Strategy Builder
*   **Type:** Drag and Drop (Timeline)
*   **Task:** Build your 60-minute timeline.
    *   [Read] [Plan] [Write] [Edit] -> Place them on the clock.

### Activity 2: The "Emergency" Kit
*   **Type:** Flashcards
*   **Task:** "What do you do if..."
    *   *Scenario:* "You run out of time." -> *Action:* "Focus on finishing the Thesis and Topic Sentences."
    *   *Scenario:* "You can't find the date." -> *Action:* "Use (n.d.) if truly missing, but check the top/bottom of page first."

### Activity 3: Final Pledge
*   **Type:** Checkbox
*   **Task:** "I am ready."
    *   [ ] I have practiced.
    *   [ ] I have reviewed my feedback.
    *   [ ] I will do my best.

## Key Takeaway
"Trust your preparation. You have done the work (15 Lessons!). Now go show what you can do."
