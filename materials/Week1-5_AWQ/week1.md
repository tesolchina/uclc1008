# Week 1: Academic Journal Articles & Reading Strategies
## HTML Files to Prepare

---

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the structure of academic journal articles
- Distinguish between empirical and conceptual articles
- Analyze titles and abstracts effectively
- Examine section headings to understand article structure
- Identify topic sentences and purpose statements
