# 15-Lesson Plan: Academic Writing Quiz (AWQ) Preparation
## Condensed 5-Week Schedule (3 Lessons Per Week)

---

## Overview
This plan condenses the original AWQ preparation materials into **15 high-impact interactive HTML lessons** (3 per week). Each lesson is designed to be self-contained yet connected, following the "Learn -> Practice -> Check" model demonstrated in `sample_paraphrasing.html`.

**Core Requirements:**
- **Total Files:** 15 HTML files
- **Week 4 Focus:** Intensive Practice
- **Week 5 Focus:** Feedback & Final Mock
- **Format:** Interactive HTML with concept notes, MC questions, and self-check writing tasks.

---

## Weekly Breakdown

### Week 1: Decoding Academic Articles
**Focus:** Understanding structure and reading efficiently.

*   **Lesson 1.1: Anatomy of an Article**
    *   **Topics:** Empirical vs. Conceptual articles, Title analysis, Abstract structure.
    *   **Activities:** Identifying article parts, analyzing titles for keywords, deconstructing an abstract.
    *   **File:** `week1_lesson1_structure.html`

*   **Lesson 1.2: Reading with Purpose**
    *   **Topics:** Section headings, Topic sentences, Purpose statements.
    *   **Activities:** Matching headings to content, locating topic sentences, identifying the writer's purpose.
    *   **File:** `week1_lesson2_reading.html`

*   **Lesson 1.3: Abstract Analysis Lab**
    *   **Topics:** Deep dive into abstract components (Context, Gap, Purpose, Method, Results, Conclusion).
    *   **Activities:** Highlighting specific moves in sample abstracts, predicting article content from the abstract.
    *   **File:** `week1_lesson3_abstracts.html`

---

### Week 2: Citation & Paraphrasing Mechanics
**Focus:** Avoiding plagiarism and mastering the mechanics of rewriting.

*   **Lesson 2.1: APA 7th Citation Bootcamp**
    *   **Topics:** In-text citations (Author-prominent vs. Information-prominent), Reference list basics, Plagiarism awareness.
    *   **Activities:** Correcting citation errors, formatting references, identifying plagiarism risks.
    *   **File:** `week2_lesson1_citations.html`

*   **Lesson 2.2: Paraphrasing Strategies (Core)**
    *   **Topics:** Synonyms, Word Forms, Voice, Sentence Patterns.
    *   **Activities:** Concept checks, strategy-specific rewriting drills (similar to `sample_paraphrasing.html`).
    *   **File:** `week2_lesson2_paraphrasing.html`

*   **Lesson 2.3: Integrated Writing Practice**
    *   **Topics:** Combining paraphrasing with citations, "Voice" management.
    *   **Activities:** rewriting short paragraphs and adding correct citations, checking "overlap" with original text.
    *   **File:** `week2_lesson3_integration.html`

---

### Week 3: Summarising & Synthesising
**Focus:** Distilling information and combining sources.

*   **Lesson 3.1: The Art of Summarising**
    *   **Topics:** Identifying main arguments vs. supporting details, Neutrality, Conciseness.
    *   **Activities:** Selecting key points from a text, deleting redundant info, writing a 1-sentence summary.
    *   **File:** `week3_lesson1_summarising.html`

*   **Lesson 3.2: Synthesis & Outlining**
    *   **Topics:** Connecting ideas from multiple sources, Building a hierarchical outline.
    *   **Activities:** Grouping similar points from different texts, arranging points logically.
    *   **File:** `week3_lesson2_synthesis.html`

*   **Lesson 3.3: AWQ Structure Prep**
    *   **Topics:** The specific AWQ prompt format, Planning the 300-word summary.
    *   **Activities:** Analyzing a sample AWQ prompt, creating a skeleton outline for a response.
    *   **File:** `week3_lesson3_awq_structure.html`

---

### Week 4: Intensive Practice Session
**Focus:** Speed, accuracy, and putting it all together.

*   **Lesson 4.1: Speed Drills (Review)**
    *   **Topics:** Rapid-fire review of Week 1-3 concepts.
    *   **Activities:** Timed MC questions on citations/reading, quick paraphrase fixes.
    *   **File:** `week4_lesson1_review.html`

*   **Lesson 4.2: Timed Practice Session (Simulation)**
    *   **Topics:** Simulated mini-AWQ tasks under time pressure.
    *   **Activities:** 20-minute timed writing task (Summarise 2 short texts), self-timing logs.
    *   **File:** `week4_lesson2_practice.html`

*   **Lesson 4.3: Self-Correction Lab**
    *   **Topics:** Analyzing the Lesson 4.2 output.
    *   **Activities:** Checklist-based review of own writing (Check citations, Check structure, Check word count).
    *   **File:** `week4_lesson3_analysis.html`

---

### Week 5: Feedback & Final Readiness
**Focus:** Mock exams, feedback analysis, and final polish.

*   **Lesson 5.1: Full Mock AWQ**
    *   **Topics:** Complete 60-minute test simulation.
    *   **Activities:** Full reading and writing task (can be broken into steps for the HTML format).
    *   **File:** `week5_lesson1_mock.html`

*   **Lesson 5.2: Feedback Session**
    *   **Topics:** Common pitfalls, "Good vs. Better" examples.
    *   **Activities:** Analyzing sample answers (identifying why one is a "B" and another is an "A"), revising a weak paragraph based on feedback.
    *   **File:** `week5_lesson2_feedback.html`

*   **Lesson 5.3: Final Readiness & Checklist**
    *   **Topics:** Final tips, Time management strategy, Stress check.
    *   **Activities:** "Go/No-Go" readiness checklist, final 5-minute refresher on top 3 errors to avoid.
    *   **File:** `week5_lesson3_final.html`
