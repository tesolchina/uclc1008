**Based on Academic Writing Quiz Instructions**

**AWQ Requirements:**
- No more than 300 words
- Introduction (background + thesis statement)
- Body paragraph(s) (topic sentences)
- Conclusion
- APA in-text citations (no abstracts, no reference list)
